Daunne Agro Farm
Daunne Agro Farm Ltd. – Nawalpur, Nepal

Introduction:

Daunne Agro Farm Ltd. located in Sardi, Binayi Tribeni-3 Nawalpur stands today as one of Nepal’s leading poultry farms specializing in layer farming. Under the leadership of Binod Pokharel the farm has grown significantly in scale automation and impact. It plays a major role in the local and national egg supply chain and has recently moved toward public investment through an IPO.

Establishment:

Established in 2017. Daunne Agro Farm began with around 100,000 layer chickens. As of early 2025, the farm has expanded to a production capacity of over 400,000 chickens producing nearly 350,000 eggs daily. The management aims to increase this to 600,000 chickens soon making it one of the largest fully automated poultry farms in the country.

Technology and Quality:

The farm uses advanced automation technology imported from Germany and Malaysia. Feeding, lighting, egg collection and waste management systems are fully automated. Eggs are packaged under the “Daunne Happy Egg” brand commonly in 15 and 30-egg cartons, ensuring hygiene, uniformity, and market readiness.

Financial Strategy:

In a landmark step Daunne Agro Farm Ltd. has launched a Rs. 100 million Initial Public Offering (IPO) in collaboration with Citizens Capital Ltd. inviting public investment. The move aims to raise funds for further expansion, automation and regional distribution. The IPO marks a major transition from private to public participation in agri-based entrepreneurship in Nepal.

Market Strategy:

In Nepal, egg consumption drops during Shravan (a religious month) and rebounds in Bhadra. Responding to this trend, Daunne Agro alongside the Nepal Egg Producers Association set standard pricing in Bhadra 2081 at:

  • Rs. 500 per crate (30 eggs)
  • Large eggs: Rs. 495
  • Medium eggs: Rs. 425

Industry Challenges:

According to Pokharel over 75% of small poultry farmers in Nepal had to shut down temporarily due to losses in recent years. The egg production rate fell from 260,000–270,000 per day to around 230,000–240,000. Despite these industry-wide challenges Daunne Agro maintained operations helped stabilize supply and pushed for fair pricing to avoid market manipulation.

Future Plans:

Expand capacity to 600,000 chickens. Produce 600 cartons of eggs daily. Integrate ERP software and complete automation. Broaden brand visibility and national supply chain. Educate and employ locals in poultry tech and farm management.

Conclusion:

Daunne Agro Farm Ltd. under Binod Pokharel’s leadership is more than just a poultry business—it is a symbol of modern agribusiness in Nepal. With bold steps like public investment, price regulation and automation. The farm is redefining what’s possible in rural entrepreneurship. Its contribution to food security, employment and innovation sets a benchmark for future agricultural ventures in Nepal.

Kisan Dana Udhyog – Tandi, Chitwan

Introduction:

Kisan Dana Udhyog Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted name in Nepal’s animal feed industry, based in Tandi, Chitwan. With a growing reputation for quality and reliability, the company produces scientifically formulated poultry feed for both layer and broiler chickens. It serves as a key supplier for poultry farmers in Chitwan, Nawalpur, and surrounding districts.

Products and Services:

The company manufactures various poultry feed categories such as:

  • Layer Starter (L1), Grower (L2–L4), Pre-layer (PL)
  • Broiler Pre-Starter, Starter, and Finisher Feed

These are developed with balanced nutrients to promote better health, egg production, and growth performance. Feed is distributed in both mash and pellet forms to suit farmer needs.

Location Advantage:

Located in Ratnanagar Municipality–12, Tandi, the factory is strategically placed with access to major transport routes. This ensures timely delivery of raw materials and finished goods. Surrounded by poultry hubs, the company maintains strong local client relationships and quick service.

Financial & Industrial Status:

As of July 2023, ICRA Nepal assigned the company a [ICRA]NP LBB-/A4 credit rating. While still growing in scale, this rating highlights its stable operations and potential to expand. The company has demonstrated financial discipline, with plans to grow distribution and production capacities.

Leadership and Workforce:

Kisan Dana Udhyog is supported by a competent management team. Public records list CFO Puru Silwal and interns such as Ashim Pokharel, reflecting both experience and youth-driven innovation. The team actively engages with farmers to improve feed efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Future Vision:

  • Expand manufacturing capacity across provinces
  • Invest in digital feed ordering and tracking
  • Introduce medicated and organic feed lines
  • Strengthen dealer networks and transport efficiency

Conclusion:

Kisan Dana Udhyog Pvt. Ltd. is playing a crucial role in improving Nepal’s poultry feed sector. With a focus on quality, efficiency, and customer support, it has become a go-to choice for farmers. As the company looks ahead to technological upgrades and market expansion, its future remains promising and impactful in Nepal’s agricultural development.
