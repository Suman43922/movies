Kishan Dana Udhyog
Kisan Dana Udhyog – Tandi, Chitwan

Introduction:

Kisan Dana Udhyog Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted name in Nepal’s animal feed industry, based in Tandi, Chitwan. With a growing reputation for quality and reliability, the company produces scientifically formulated poultry feed for both layer and broiler chickens. It serves as a key supplier for poultry farmers in Chitwan, Nawalpur, and surrounding districts.

Products and Services:

The company manufactures various poultry feed categories such as:

  • Layer Starter (L1), Grower (L2–L4), Pre-layer (PL)
  • Broiler Pre-Starter, Starter, and Finisher Feed

These are developed with balanced nutrients to promote better health, egg production, and growth performance. Feed is distributed in both mash and pellet forms to suit farmer needs.

Location Advantage:

Located in Ratnanagar Municipality–12, Tandi, the factory is strategically placed with access to major transport routes. This ensures timely delivery of raw materials and finished goods. Surrounded by poultry hubs, the company maintains strong local client relationships and quick service.

Financial & Industrial Status:

As of July 2023, ICRA Nepal assigned the company a [ICRA]NP LBB-/A4 credit rating. While still growing in scale, this rating highlights its stable operations and potential to expand. The company has demonstrated financial discipline, with plans to grow distribution and production capacities.

Leadership and Workforce:

Kisan Dana Udhyog is supported by a competent management team. Public records list CFO Puru Silwal and interns such as Ashim Pokharel, reflecting both experience and youth-driven innovation. The team actively engages with farmers to improve feed efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Future Vision:

  • Expand manufacturing capacity across provinces
  • Invest in digital feed ordering and tracking
  • Introduce medicated and organic feed lines
  • Strengthen dealer networks and transport efficiency

Conclusion:

Kisan Dana Udhyog Pvt. Ltd. is playing a crucial role in improving Nepal’s poultry feed sector. With a focus on quality, efficiency, and customer support, it has become a go-to choice for farmers. As the company looks ahead to technological upgrades and market expansion, its future remains promising and impactful in Nepal’s agricultural development.
