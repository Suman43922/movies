Movies Review - Collie

🎬 Movie Overview:

Director: Lokesh Kanagaraj

Producer: Kalanithi Maran (Sun Pictures)

Language: Tamil (with dubbed versions in Telugu, Kannada, Hindi)

Release Date: August 14, 2025

Budget: ₹350–375 Crore (One of India’s costliest films)

Genre: Action-Thriller

🎭 Cast:

Rajinikanth – as Deva, the lead character

Nagarjuna – as Simon

Upendra – as Kelisha

Soubin Shahir – as Dayal

Satya Raj, Shruti Haasan, Reba Monica John, Monisha Blessy, Kalaiyarasan, Jr. MGR

Pooja Hegde – special dance number cameo

Aamir Khan – surprise cameo as Daha

📖 Storyline:

Coolie revolves around Deva, a man with a mysterious past who rises from being a porter to someone feared by the underground world.

He seeks revenge against a shadowy enemy tied to his childhood, fighting for justice, redemption, and to protect the poor and voiceless.

The film explores themes of justice, power, betrayal, and redemption wrapped in mass-action sequences.

Director Lokesh clarified that Coolie is not part of his LCU (Lokesh Cinematic Universe) – it’s a standalone cinematic spectacle.

🛠 Technical Crew:

Cinematography: Girish Gangadharan

Editing: Philomin Raj

Music: Anirudh Ravichander – known for high-energy scores and emotional ballads

Shooting Locations: Chennai, Hyderabad, Visakhapatnam, Jaipur, Bangkok

Status: Filming completed in early July 2025
